What FoodPeel Is (and Isn't)

FoodPeel is an informational tool. We scan barcodes, look up ingredients, and provide safety ratings based on publicly available research and regulatory data.

FoodPeel is not medical advice. Our ratings are not diagnoses, prescriptions, or medical recommendations. If you have a specific medical condition, allergy, or dietary restriction, please consult a qualified healthcare professional. FoodPeel is a starting point, not a substitute for professional guidance.

How Our Ratings Work

Every FoodPeel rating is based on:

  • Publicly available scientific research and peer-reviewed studies
  • Regulatory decisions from the FDA, EU food safety agencies, and equivalent bodies
  • Published ingredient safety databases

We do our best to keep ratings current. However, food science evolves. Ingredients may be studied further, regulatory stances may change, and new products hit shelves every day. We update our database regularly, but we cannot guarantee that every rating reflects the absolute latest scientific consensus at every moment.

If you believe a rating is incorrect or outdated, please let us know at hello@foodpeel.com. We take accuracy seriously and will review promptly.
