The Problem

Reading food labels shouldn't
require a PhD.

The food industry has spent decades making it harder — not easier — to know what's in your food. It's time that changed.

😩

You can't pronounce half the ingredients

Butylated hydroxyanisole. Carrageenan. Polysorbate 80. These aren't just hard to say — some of them are hard on your body. You deserve to know what they are before you eat them.

🌍

Stuff that's banned in Europe is legal here

The EU has banned over 1,300 chemicals from food products. The US has banned fewer than 20. Popular American snacks, cereals, and drinks contain additives that are flat-out illegal in most of the developed world.

⏰

You don't have time to research every label

The average grocery run involves 50+ product choices. Nobody has time to Google every ingredient on every label while their kids are running down the aisle. You need answers in seconds — not a research project.
