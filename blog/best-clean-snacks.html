The 20 Cleanest Snacks
You Can Actually Buy

Most "healthy" snacks are just junk food with better marketing. But there are genuinely clean packaged snacks available at your regular grocery store — short ingredient lists, no artificial additives, no hidden junk. Here are the ones that pass a FoodPeel check.

Our Selection Criteria

Every snack on this list was chosen based on these standards:

✓ Short ingredient list
Usually under 5 ingredients. You should recognize everything on the list.
✓ No artificial additives
No synthetic dyes, artificial preservatives, or artificial sweeteners.
✓ No "natural flavors" mystery
Where flavors are used, they're clearly identified. No ingredient-list hiding.
✓ Widely available
Found at major grocery chains — not just Whole Foods or specialty stores.

These aren't necessarily the most nutritionally optimal choices. They're real-world packaged snacks that pass a clean ingredient check. We're not perfect food extremists here — we're people who want to make better choices without turning grocery shopping into a full-time job.

The 20 Cleanest Packaged Snacks

01
Larabar (Original Flavors)

The cleanest bar on the mass market. The original flavors are remarkable for how few ingredients they contain. The cherry pie bar has literally two ingredients.

Cherry Pie ingredients: dates, cherries. That's it.
Find it at: Virtually every grocery store, Target, CVS
02
RX Bar (Original)

Built its brand on ingredient transparency — the main ingredients are listed on the front of the wrapper. Egg whites, nuts, and dates. No B.S.

Chocolate Sea Salt: dates, egg whites, almonds, cashews, chocolate, sea salt
Find it at: Target, Walmart, most grocery stores
03
Siete Grain-Free Tortilla Chips

Made from cassava flour and avocado oil. Grain-free without the junk-filled ingredient list that plagues most "alternative" snacks. Clean and actually tasty.

Sea Salt: cassava flour, avocado oil, sea salt
Find it at: Target, Whole Foods, Sprouts, many major chains
04
Justin's Almond Butter Packets

Single-serve almond butter with nothing added but a pinch of sea salt. Portable protein and fat with zero ingredient surprises.

Classic Almond Butter: dry-roasted almonds, palm oil, sea salt
Find it at: Target, Walmart, Costco, most grocery stores
05
SkinnyPop Original Popcorn

Three ingredients. Nothing else. One of the cleanest mass-market snacks available and genuinely widely available.

Original: popcorn, sunflower oil, salt
Find it at: Everywhere — this is one of the most widely distributed clean snacks
06
Chomps Grass-Fed Meat Sticks

The cleanest meat snack available. Grass-fed beef or turkey, no antibiotics, no nitrates. A legitimate protein snack without the jerky ingredient wall.

Original Beef: beef, water, sea salt, vinegar, lemon juice, celery powder, black pepper, garlic powder
Find it at: Target, Whole Foods, Sprouts, online
07
Annie's Organic Cheddar Bunnies

Organic wheat flour, organic cheddar, some basic baking ingredients. No artificial dyes, no synthetic preservatives. A legitimately cleaner cracker for the cracker aisle.

Cheddar Bunnies: organic wheat flour, organic cheddar cheese, organic butter, organic cornstarch, sea salt, organic onion powder, natural cheddar flavor
Find it at: Target, Walmart, most grocery stores
08
Bare Baked Crunchy Apple Chips

Sliced apples. Baked. That's the entire product. Whole fruit in chip form with nothing added. Better than you'd expect.

Fuji & Reds: apples. One ingredient.
Find it at: Target, Walmart, Kroger, most major grocery chains
09
Simple Mills Almond Flour Crackers

Almond flour base instead of refined wheat. More protein, more fat, less blood sugar spike. The ingredient list reads like real food.

Sea Salt: almond flour, cassava flour, sunflower oil, flaxseed, sunflower seeds, sea salt, rosemary extract
Find it at: Target, Whole Foods, Sprouts, Thrive Market
10
Primal Kitchen Collagen Bars

Nut and collagen-based bar with clean ingredients. No artificial sweeteners — sweetened with honey and dates. Whole30 approved.

Dark Chocolate Almond: almonds, collagen peptides, dark chocolate, sunflower seed butter, honey, pumpkin seeds, sea salt
Find it at: Target, Whole Foods, Sprouts
11
Kind Bars (Original Nut Bars, Not the "Healthy Grains" Line)

The original nut bars have clean ingredients. Avoid the "Healthy Grains" and flavored varieties, which have longer and less clean ingredient lists. Stick to the classics.

Almond & Coconut: almonds, coconut, chicory root fiber, honey, palm kernel oil, sugar, rice flour, sea salt, soy lecithin
Find it at: Everywhere
12
Epic Provisions Pork Rinds

Pasture-raised pork skin with minimal seasoning. Zero carbs, high protein, legitimately clean ingredient list. Better option in the pork rind category.

Sea Salt & Pepper: pork rinds, sea salt, black pepper. Three ingredients.
Find it at: Target, Walmart, Whole Foods
13
Stacy's Pita Chips (Simply Naked)

Baked pita with a short list of recognizable baking ingredients. Not the flavored varieties — the plain version has a clean, simple label.

Simply Naked: enriched wheat flour, sunflower oil, sugar, sea salt, dried yeast, wheat starch
Find it at: Most grocery stores, Target, Walmart
14
Lesser Evil Popcorn (Himalayan Gold)

Organic popcorn popped in organic coconut oil with Himalayan salt. Better fat profile than sunflower oil and an equally short ingredient list.

Himalayan Gold: organic popcorn, organic coconut oil, Himalayan pink salt
Find it at: Whole Foods, Sprouts, Target
15
Dang Sticky Rice Chips (Original)

Thai-style sticky rice chips with a surprisingly short ingredient list. Interesting texture, clean profile, satisfying as a chip alternative.

Original: sticky rice, coconut oil, sea salt, black sesame seeds
Find it at: Whole Foods, Target, online
16
Trader Joe's Organic Dried Mango (No Sugar Added)

One ingredient: dried mango. No added sugar, no sulfur dioxide, no artificial preservatives. A genuine whole-food snack in convenient packaged form.

Dried Mango: organic mango. One ingredient.
Find it at: Trader Joe's only
17
Bob's Red Mill Classic Oatmeal Packets

The classic single-serve oatmeal — the plain version. Whole grain oats with optional cinnamon and sea salt. No sugar, no flavoring agents, no anything suspicious.

Classic Oatmeal: whole grain rolled oats
Find it at: Most grocery stores, Target, Walmart
18
Nuttzo Power Fuel (7-Nut Butter)

Seven raw nuts and seeds blended together. No added oil, no added sugar, no palm oil filler. Dense nutrition in a small jar. Best for people who want more variety than standard nut butter.

Original Crunchy: cashews, almonds, Brazil nuts, flaxseeds, hazelnuts, pumpkin seeds, chia seeds, sea salt
Find it at: Whole Foods, Sprouts, online
19
Catalina Crunch Keto Snack Mix

Lower-carb snack mix that skips the artificial sweeteners and protein isolates common in keto products. Plant-based protein, short ingredient list.

Dark Chocolate: pea protein, tapioca flour, sunflower oil, chicory root, cocoa, sea salt
Find it at: Target, Walmart, online
20
Good Culture Cottage Cheese (Simple Whole Milk)

Cottage cheese from pasture-raised cows with no gums, no thickeners, no carrageenan. The ingredient list is milk, cream, cultures, salt — and that's it. Legitimately rare in the cottage cheese category.

Simple Whole Milk: cultured pasteurized whole milk, cream, sea salt, live and active cultures
Find it at: Target, Whole Foods, major grocery chains

Important caveat: Always check the current label. Formulations change. Brands get acquired. What was clean two years ago may have an updated ingredient list with new additives. Scan with FoodPeel before buying — don't assume a brand you trusted last year hasn't changed their formula.

What to Avoid in the "Health" Snack Aisle

While these 20 snacks are solid, plenty of snacks marketed as healthy are anything but. Watch out for these specific red flags in snacks:

  • Chicory root fiber in quantity — used to inflate fiber numbers on labels; causes digestive distress in many people
  • Carrageenan — seaweed-derived emulsifier linked to gut inflammation; common in "natural" dairy products
  • Protein isolates as the first ingredient — heavily processed, often with hexane extraction; not a real food protein source
  • Multiple artificial sweeteners stacked — a product with sucralose AND acesulfame potassium AND stevia has been heavily engineered
  • Maltodextrin as a main ingredient — highly processed starch with a glycemic index higher than table sugar
  • Fractionated palm kernel oil — highly saturated fat that appears in snacks to avoid tropical oils but is no better

The best snacks are the ones that don't need a complicated ingredient list because the ingredients themselves are the food. When you see a snack that's mostly nuts, seeds, whole fruit, or simple grains with nothing added, you've found something worth buying.
