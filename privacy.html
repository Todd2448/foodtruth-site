Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy.
Seriously.

No dark patterns, no hidden data selling, no surprise marketing. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

Effective date: April 2026

🔒 Short version: We collect your email to keep you updated about FoodPeel. We never sell your data. Ever. You can ask us to delete everything, anytime.

What We Collect

We only collect what we actually need to run FoodPeel:

  • Email address — collected when you sign up for the waitlist or create an account. Used to send you launch updates and product news. That's it.
  • Scan data — when you scan a product, we log which products were scanned to improve our database. This data is fully anonymized — it is never linked to your identity.
  • Usage analytics — basic, anonymized data about how the app is used (e.g., which features are popular, crash reports). We use this to improve the product. No PII is collected or stored.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name (unless you choose to provide it)
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your purchase history or financial information
  • Any data from your contacts, camera roll, or other apps

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. This is a hard line for us — not a legal hedge.

If that ever changes (it won't), we will notify you directly and give you a clear opt-out before anything changes.

How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. We use reputable third-party services (e.g., Formspree for form handling) that have their own privacy and security practices.

We do not store payment information ourselves — all payments are handled through Stripe, which is PCI-compliant.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data — just ask
  • Unsubscribe from any emails at any time via the unsubscribe link

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@foodpeel.com. We'll respond within 48 hours.

Cookies

FoodPeel's website uses minimal cookies — only what's needed for the site to function (e.g., form submission state). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party ad cookies.

Children's Privacy

FoodPeel is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at privacy@foodpeel.com and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we'll notify you via email before the changes take effect. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
