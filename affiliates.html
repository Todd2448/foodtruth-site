Common Questions

When does the affiliate program go live?
The program launches simultaneously with the FoodPeel app. We're accepting applications now so we can onboard partners ahead of launch — early applicants get priority access, dedicated support during launch, and the best chance to earn the first wave of commissions.
How long do commissions last per referral?
Your commissions are recurring — you earn 20-30% of whatever your referrals pay, every month they remain subscribed. There's no cap and no expiration. A referral you make today could still be earning you money two years from now.
What if someone signs up on mobile vs. desktop?
Your referral link works on any device. The cookie window (30-90 days depending on tier) tracks the user regardless of which device they eventually subscribe on, as long as they clicked your link within that window.
Do I need to be a FoodPeel subscriber to be an affiliate?
We strongly recommend it — the best affiliates actually use and believe in what they're recommending. We'll provide a complimentary subscription to approved affiliates who want one before the public launch.
Can I promote FoodPeel in paid ads?
Ambassador and Partner tier affiliates may not bid on FoodPeel brand keywords in paid search. Content promotion, sponsored social posts, email newsletters, and influencer campaigns are all permitted. Agency tier has more flexibility — discuss with your account manager.
