The Origin Story
"It started with a cereal box."

Todd was standing in the grocery aisle, trying to figure out if a cereal was safe for his kid. He flipped it over. Read the ingredient list. Found something he didn't recognize. Googled it on his phone.

The results were conflicting. One site said it was fine. Another said it was linked to cancer. A third required a subscription to read. Twenty minutes later, he still didn't have a clear answer — and his kids were running laps around the cart.

He put the cereal back, grabbed a different one, and thought: there has to be a better way.

There wasn't. So he built one.

That frustrating Tuesday in the grocery aisle became FoodPeel — an app that does in three seconds what used to take twenty minutes of frustrated Googling. Clean, clear, science-backed answers. Right when you need them.
