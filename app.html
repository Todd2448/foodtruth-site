Quick Questions

Is FoodPeel free?
The core app — barcode scanning and ingredient ratings — will be free. Premium features (family profiles, product history, advanced alternatives) will be part of a low-cost subscription. Early access members lock in founding pricing.
What phones will it work on?
iOS 15+ and Android 10+. Any phone with a working camera and rear-facing autofocus will be supported.
How big is the product database?
We're launching with over 100,000 products and thousands of ingredients. Growing daily. Products not in the database can be manually searched by ingredient list.
What if I find a product you don't have?
You can manually enter any ingredient list and get instant ratings. You can also flag missing products — we prioritize adding products requested by our users.
