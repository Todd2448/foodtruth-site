FAQ

Common Questions

When will FoodPeel launch?
We're in final development for iOS and Android. Sign up for early access and you'll be first to know — plus you'll lock in 3 free months of Pro when we launch.
Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes. The free tier is free forever — no credit card required, no time limit. You get 10 scans per month and basic ratings. If you want unlimited scans and deeper ingredient breakdowns, that's what Pro is for.
How does FoodPeel get its ingredient data?
We combine barcode databases, manufacturer ingredient lists, and peer-reviewed research on food additives to build our safety ratings. Our team reviews and updates ratings regularly as new research emerges.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account settings. If you cancel mid-cycle, you keep access through the end of your billing period. No penalties, no runaround.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, contact us and we'll make it right. We'd rather keep you happy than keep your money.
What's included in early access?
Early access members get: first look at the app before public launch, 3 free months of Pro at launch, the ability to influence features before we ship them, and a guaranteed spot when the app goes live.
